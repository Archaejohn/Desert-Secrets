# Desert Secrets — Act 2: "The Ice Below" (~30–40 min)

Joseph descends through the crack in the mine wall into the impossible
glacier under the Mojave. Act 2 is a **maze act**: winding ice tunnels,
rooms with multiple exits, false leads that loop back or dead-end — some
dead ends hide treasure and lost miners, one hides a friend.

## Cast (new)

| Character | Role |
|---|---|
| **Slither** | A jade whipsnake who's lived under the ice for years. Shy at first, scouts shortcuts no human fits through, and **joins the party** before the act's boss. Speech pattern: sssoft hissing, dry wit. |
| **Mo, Edda & Gus** | Lost miners from the Cinnabar crew that "vanished" years ago. Each is stranded down a different false lead. Rescuing all three earns a perk and pries open the sanctum door faster — and they've *smelled tomato pie* down here (Act 5 seed). |
| **The Rime Warden** | Act 2 boss: an ancient beetle-construct sheathed in ice, guarding the frozen lake. Not evil — wound up, still following the last order it was given. |
| **Fluffball** (cameo) | At the very end, TWO penguin silhouettes cross the cracking lake ice — Piggy has a friend down here: a round, gray, extremely fluffy chick. They vanish into a tunnel together. |

## Structure (four zones)

1. **The Crevasse** (`crevasse`) — entry hall under the Depths. Teaches the
   act's grammar: three ways out, one is a loop, one is a dead-end pocket
   holding **Mo** (dead ends can pay off). Radio is dead down here — the
   objective line carries wayfinding.
2. **The Ice Maze** (`maze`) — the big one: ≥6 rooms, corridors with
   multiple doorways, at least two true routes to the far side, ≥3 false
   leads (one loops back to the entry room; dead ends hold a heal-shard
   cache, **Edda**, and an ambush). A too-narrow crack blocks a shortcut —
   **Slither** peeks out here: talk to him and he slips through and opens
   it from the other side. Random encounters: frost scarabs, ice bats.
3. **The Galleries** (`galleries`) — frozen-over mine works. **Gus** is
   trapped down a side gallery. The far door is sealed with rime; Slither
   squeezes through its workings and unbolts it — and, having decided
   Joseph is warm and mostly harmless, **joins the party** ("Sssomebody
   has to keep you alive."). Random encounters: ice bats, crystal crawlers.
4. **The Sanctum** (`sanctum`) — the frozen lake. Boss: the **Rime
   Warden**, fought with the full two-member party (Joseph + Slither).
   Then the ending: the lake ice cracks in a line, two penguin shapes
   skitter across — Piggy and the fluffy stranger — and dive into a far
   tunnel. Slither: "...Two? There are TWO?" End card: **ACT 3: THE
   SUNLESS SEA**.

## Party mechanics (new this act)

- **Slither in battle**: second ATB gauge. Commands: **Bite** (attack),
  **Coil** (guard), **Venom** (0.75× damage + slows the target's ATB speed
  by 25%, stacking down to 50%). His HP scales with Joseph's level and he
  recovers fully between battles (snakes shed everything, including bad
  days).
- **Leveling continues**: thresholds extend to Level 8 (150/195/245). The
  act is tuned to carry a Level 4–5 hero to Level 6–7 by the Warden.
- Defeat still returns the party to the start of the current area with
  everything kept.

## Objective chain

Descend → find a way through the maze (rescue whoever you find) → open the
rime door → the lake. Side objective: "Miners rescued: N/3" — all three =
bonus perk + they crowbar the sanctum door's counterweight (skips a fight).

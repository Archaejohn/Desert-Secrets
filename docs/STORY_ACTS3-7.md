# Desert Secrets — Acts 3–7: The Ingredient Chase ("Part One" finale)

The back half of the game answers one question: **how do you catch a
penguin who doesn't want to be caught?** Answer: you learn what he loves,
you go get it — four separate times, four separate places — and you cook
it. Catching Piggy at the end of Act 7 closes out **Part One** of Desert
Secrets. What comes after is planned in
[`docs/STORY_PARTS2-4.md`](./STORY_PARTS2-4.md) — Part One's finale below
sets it up directly: the walk out doesn't go the way anyone expects.

## The shape of it

- **One ingredient, one act, one place.** No collect-quest act that
  reuses old maps for a checklist — each of the four things Piggy loves
  (fish, stinky socks, oranges, seaweed) gets its own new location, cast,
  and reason to exist.
- **Piggy is chased in every single act.** He's not offstage between
  ingredient beats — each act has its own near-catch set piece, and each
  one fails a little differently, so the chase itself has an arc: cute
  and comic early, then genuinely tense by Act 6.
- **Fluffball hands over exactly one clue per act** — never more. He's
  spotted at a distance in Acts 3–4 (too shy/scared to approach), then
  actually **joins the party in Act 5**, so he's traveling with Joseph
  and Slither for the final two ingredient acts (5 and 6) before the
  Act 7 finale.
- **Piggy is finally caught in Act 7** — not chased down, but drawn in
  by smell, mid-bite, once the pizza actually exists.

| Act | Ingredient | Place | Fluffball | New mechanic |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 3 | Fish | The Sunless Sea | glimpsed, gives clue #1, won't approach | floe-hopping traversal + fishing minigame |
| 4 | Stinky socks | The Miners' Camp | glimpsed again, clue #2, still won't approach | favor-quest + a "reeks" inventory item that changes NPC/enemy behavior |
| 5 | Oranges | Sahra's Hidden Grove | **joins the party**, clue #3 | dialogue that reacts to Act 1–2 choices; first real bonding scene |
| 6 | Seaweed | The Reef | traveling with the party, clue #4 | trade-not-fight diplomacy (Slither + Fluffball broker it) |
| 7 | — (the pizza) | La Pizzeria Sotterranea | full party member | cooking/timing minigame; the actual catch |

## Act 3 — "The Sunless Sea" (~30–35 min)

Joseph and Slither follow the crack Piggy vanished through into a
bioluminescent cavern ocean under the glacier — mint-glow reefs, kelp
forests, drifting ice floes. New traversal: hopping floe to floe, poled
along by Slither, who swims ahead as scout.

- **The chase:** Piggy is spotted playing tag with something small and
  gray far off across the water. Every approach fails because the
  penguins are simply faster in water than Joseph is on a raft — comic,
  low-stakes misses.
- **Fluffball, glimpsed:** cornered once, briefly, in a dead-end kelp
  bed — and bolts the second Joseph gets close, dropping (or shrieking)
  one clue as he goes: Piggy's favorite fish, **silverfin**, only runs
  in the sea's deepest kelp beds, past where the light gives out.
- Mid-act: the flooded ruins of the sun-temple Sahra hinted at back in
  Act 1 — the desert wasn't hiding treasure, it was hiding an
  **ecosystem** that's been down here since the Mojave was ocean.
- **Fishing minigame:** a timing-based catch off a floe for the silverfin
  clue; a much bigger fish (mini-boss: **the Lurker**) keeps stealing the
  lure and has to be fought off before the real catch works.

**End card: ACT 4 — THE MINERS' CAMP.**

## Act 4 — "Dirty Laundry" (~30 min)

Back up through the galleries to where Mo, Edda and Gus actually live
now — a scrappy little camp built into an abandoned gallery, string
lights, a laundry line. New location, not a revisit of Act 2's map.

- **The chase:** Piggy's been raiding the camp at night — footprints in
  the frost, a stolen boot. He's spotted sniffing around the supply
  crates; the near-catch fails when he burrows into a crate stack and
  vanishes out the far side before Joseph can dig him out.
- **Fluffball, glimpsed again:** watching from a ledge, still won't come
  down. Clue #2: Piggy loves the **ripest** socks in camp, not just any
  socks — and the miners won't just hand those over.
- **Favor-quest:** Mo, Edda and Gus want a nest of camp pests cleared out
  of the laundry nook first (new enemy: **midden mites**, small and
  numerous, first fight that rewards AOE-ish play). Payoff: the socks.
- **New system:** stinky socks become a genuine inventory item — carrying
  them changes how NPCs and some enemies react (comic dialogue reactions;
  a couple of enemy types actively avoid the party while they're held).

**End card: ACT 5 — SAHRA'S GROVE.**

## Act 5 — "The Hidden Grove" (~30 min)

Back to the surface — a tonal breather after two underground/aquatic
acts. Sahra keeps a small hidden orange grove behind the oasis, watered
by the same spring.

- **The chase:** Piggy beat them here — he's already in the grove,
  snacking. The near-catch fails when he waddle-sprints into a patch of
  needle-cactus too dense for Joseph to follow, and for the first time
  it isn't funny for a second — he's scared, not just playing.
- **Fluffball joins:** shaken by that moment, Fluffball finally comes all
  the way in instead of clue-and-run, decides Joseph's trying to *help*
  Piggy, not just catch him, and sticks around from here on — riding in
  the pack, commenting, present in camp scenes and battles as a
  non-combat companion (Act 6+ decides if he ever fights).
- **Fluffball, clue #3** (now given properly, in conversation, not
  shouted while fleeing): the **oranges**, but only the ones Sahra grows
  in the grove's oldest row.
- **Sahra's price:** she trades oranges for news, and her dialogue here
  reacts to what actually happened in Acts 1–2 (cold pack kept or
  traded; Queen fought or parleyed) — first real callback payoff.

**End card: ACT 6 — THE REEF.**

## Act 6 — "The Reef" (~30–35 min)

Back underground and underwater, to the crystal-crawler territory glimpsed
in Act 2's galleries — their actual home, a garden of glowing kelp they
farm.

- **The chase, and the turn:** this is the last ingredient before the
  finale, so the near-catch here has to land harder than any before it.
  They finally corner Piggy for real — and he's frightened, not playful,
  the first time the chase stops being cute. He slips through a gap too
  small for Joseph and is gone, and it's Fluffball, not Joseph, who calls
  after him. Raises the stakes going into Act 7: this isn't a game to
  Piggy either.
- **Fluffball, clue #4:** the exact **seaweed** — a mint kelp variety the
  crawlers cultivate on purpose, not wild growth.
- **Trade, not a fight:** the crawlers are territorial but not hostile;
  getting the seaweed is a diplomacy sequence — Slither negotiates,
  Fluffball translates/vouches (established here as able to get through
  to reef life a little), and a bad approach turns it into an avoidable
  fight instead of an instant one.

**End card: ACT 7 — LA PIZZERIA SOTTERRANEA.**

## Act 7 — "La Pizzeria Sotterranea" (~30–35 min)

Deep beneath everything, where the miners swore they smelled tomato pie
(Act 2 seed, paid off): a mysterious underground pizza restaurant carved
into the temple's old kitchens, run by **Chef Testudo** — an ancient
tortoise who has been perfecting one recipe since the Mojave had a
coastline. Lit by lava vents; the tables are set for guests who stopped
coming three thousand years ago.

- **The bake:** a cooking/timing minigame assembling the exact pizza —
  dough, tomato, the **oranges**, the **seaweed**, the **silverfin** on
  the side, the **socks** hung by the door because ambience matters to
  Piggy for reasons nobody questions anymore.
- **The catch:** the smell travels the tunnels. Piggy arrives at a
  waddling sprint — no chase, no near-miss, he just *comes*. Fluffball
  vouches. Piggy is finally, gently caught, mid-bite. It plays as a
  reunion, not a capture.
- **The reveal:** Testudo explains the secret in full. The glacier is the
  last of the old ocean, kept asleep under the sand, and it's been waking
  since the crash in Act 1. Piggy's frost was never a fluke — emperor
  penguins were the old ocean's favorites, and the ice *remembers*. It
  followed him because it wanted to go home.
- **Finale, and the Part One/Part Two hinge:** with Piggy caught, fed, and
  the secret out, Joseph, Slither, Fluffball and a very full Piggy carry a
  seed of the ice back up through the tunnels, heading for the surface
  and Rosa's radio call — mission accomplished, headed home. They never
  make it. **End of Part One** — Part Two opens mid-step, the floor gone
  out from under them. (What Piggy's future actually holds — stay, go,
  something else — isn't decided here. That question waits for the game's
  real ending, after Parts 2–4.)

## Companion notes

- **Slither** is present and battle-capable from Act 2 onward, unchanged.
- **Fluffball** is a *non-combat* companion once he joins in Act 5 — pack
  rider, camp-scene and dialogue presence, banter system — not a third
  ATB combatant. Whether he ever gets a battle role is an open question
  for later, not decided here.
- Party banter (Slither + Fluffball reacting to each other, to zones, to
  choices made earlier) is a new system this arc needs; scope it small
  first (a few lines per zone) rather than a full dialogue tree per
  location.

## Production notes

- Same pipeline discipline as Acts 1–2: palette-locked generated art,
  contracts written into `docs/CONTRACTS.md` before building, core rules
  pure + unit-tested, BFS-verified maps, a full headless e2e per act.
- New systems queue, roughly in the order they're first needed:
  swimming/floe traversal + fishing minigame (Act 3), inventory items
  that change NPC/enemy behavior (Act 4), companion banter system +
  Sahra's reactive dialogue (Act 5), trade/diplomacy-not-combat
  encounters (Act 6), cooking/timing minigame (Act 7).
- New enemies to design per act (flavor only, not stat tables yet): an
  eel or anglerfish-type predator for the Sunless Sea (Act 3); midden
  mites, small/numerous, for the miners' camp nest (Act 4); something
  guarding the grove — wasps or sunstung locusts fit the tonal breather
  (Act 5); a reef predator distinct from the (mostly peaceful)
  crystal-crawlers themselves (Act 6).
- Fluffball's ingredient list — fish, stinky socks, oranges, and seaweed
  (for the pizza) — is canon from `docs/STORY_ACT1.md`/`STORY_ACT2.md`
  forward; this document is the first place the four get split into four
  separate acts instead of one collect-quest act.

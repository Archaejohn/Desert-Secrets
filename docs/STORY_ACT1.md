# Desert Secrets — Story Bible, Act 1: "The Coldest Cargo"

**Setting:** the Mojave Desert — creosote flats, dry lakebeds, Joshua tree
groves, a derelict Route 40 gas station, and the abandoned Cinnabar Mine.
**Hero:** Joseph, a local wildlife-rescue volunteer who knows the flats
better than anyone.
**Goal of the game:** save Piggy, a baby emperor penguin, lost in the
hottest place he could possibly be.
**Act 1 length target:** ~30 minutes.

## Logline

When an aquarium transport truck jackknifes on the highway at dusk, its
smallest passenger waddles off into the Mojave. Joseph follows the trail —
and discovers that wherever Piggy rests, the desert frosts over. The ice
isn't melting. The desert has been waiting for something cold.

## Tone

Warm-hearted adventure with a thread of mystery. The desert is dangerous
but never cruel; every "monster" has a reason. Dialogue is short, dry, and
kind — that register carries the whole game.

## Cast (Act 1)

| Character | Role |
|---|---|
| **Joseph** | Player character. Practical, soft-spoken, carries a canteen and a wildlife crate harness. |
| **Piggy** | Baby emperor penguin. Cannot speak; communicates in waddles, squeaks and one devastating look. Frost follows him. |
| **Rosa Delgado** | The transport driver. Radios Joseph tips and urgency; the act's clock ("he can't take this heat much longer"). |
| **John & Pamela** | Joseph's parents, at the family homestead by the spring. Warm, practical, a little worried — about Piggy, and about Thomas being unreachable all day. Give the trail hints and an optional chicken-chores side quest. |
| **Dusty** | A giant pack rat who runs a trading post out of the Last Chance Fuel station. Trades in shiny things; saw everything. |
| **The Dust Queen** | Brood-mother of the giant scarabs nesting in Cinnabar Mine. Act 1 boss — and not a villain. |

## The secret (whole-game spine, seeded in Act 1)

Deep under the Mojave sleeps a body of ancient glacial ice — the desert's
buried secret. It is why the scarab brood digs, why the mine was abandoned,
and why frost clings to Piggy: the ice *resonates* with him. Act 1 only
shows the audience the first glimpse; explanations belong to later acts.

## Act 1 outline (five beats + cliffhanger)

### Beat 1 — The Crash (minutes 0–3)
Dusk on the highway shoulder. Rosa's truck has jackknifed; one crate is
broken open and empty. Movement/interaction tutorial: inspect the crate,
find the first **frost feather** (a penguin feather rimed with ice that
isn't melting). Rosa gives Joseph the radio and a cold pack, and the act's
stakes: Piggy is a *baby*; in desert heat he has hours, not days. A quiet
aside plants a much longer thread: Joseph hasn't heard from his fraternal
twin brother **Thomas** all day and doesn't know where he is — Thomas
doesn't resurface until Parts 2–4 (`docs/STORY_PARTS2-4.md`).
*No combat. Ends when Joseph steps off the asphalt onto the flats.*

### Beat 2 — The Homestead (minutes 3–9)
Cross Cholla Flats to the family homestead by the spring (the existing
oasis map). John saw Piggy waddle past at dawn, headed east; the ground's
been humming since the crash — the scarabs are stirring.
**Forced tutorial battle: one scarab** (teaches ATB gauges, Attack,
Guard). A quiet hub lets Joseph ask about Thomas (still missing, second
mention of the thread) or the chickens (an optional side quest — feed and
water them at the coop for bonus XP, never required). Farewell: John
mentions ice out on the flats that never melted — first hint someone
*noticed*, without Sahra's mysticism; the mystery itself stays seeded by
Rosa's crash-site line instead. Quest: follow three **ice chips** Piggy
left where he rested. *Joseph reaches Level 2 here.*

### Beat 3 — The Trail (minutes 9–17)
Three micro-zones, one clue each:
1. **Soda Dry Lake** — follow Piggy's tracks through a heat-mirage that
   only renders true reflections (light navigation puzzle).
2. **Joshua tree grove** — a jackrabbit has stolen the second ice chip;
   chase it down (optional battle *or* trade it the cold pack — first
   choice with consequences: keeping the cold pack matters in Beat 5).
3. **Last Chance Fuel** — Dusty the pack rat traded Piggy's ID tag from
   the crows and, for a shiny, tells the truth: Piggy followed the cool
   air breathing out of **Cinnabar Mine**.
2–4 battles en route (buzzards, a gila monster, scarabs). Rosa radio
check-ins mark the act clock. *Level 3 by the mine gates.*

### Beat 4 — The Mine (minutes 17–23)
Mini-dungeon: three tunnel forks, a mine-cart lever puzzle to open the
shaft elevator, 2–3 brood battles, then the mid-boss: the **Foreman
Scarab**, an armored elite guarding the descent. The deeper Joseph goes,
the *colder* it gets — frost on the rails, breath fogging. Wrong, and
wonderful. *Level 4 after the Foreman.*

### Beat 5 — The Dust Queen (minutes 23–28)
The lowest gallery: an underground spring, scarab eggs packed around it —
and Piggy, unhurt, huddled at the center, his frost keeping the clutch
alive through the killing heat. The Dust Queen will not give him up.
Player choice (existing dialogue-choice system):
- **Fight** — full boss battle against the Dust Queen.
- **Parley** — if Joseph kept the cold pack, he can offer it for the eggs;
  the fight becomes a shorter scripted skirmish when the mine trembles
  mid-negotiation. (Either path yields the boss XP; parley is the
  "clever" route, not the punished one.)

### Cliffhanger — The Ice Below (minutes 28–30)
Joseph carries Piggy to the elevator. An aftershock splits the gallery
wall — revealing a sheer face of **blue glacial ice**, impossibly, under
the Mojave — with something vast and dark suspended inside. The frost
feather in Joseph's pocket pulses. Piggy squirms free and waddles *toward*
the ice as the elevator cage crashes down behind them, sealing the way
back. **Title card: Act 2 — The Ice Below.**

## XP & leveling system

### Rules
- XP comes from **battles**, **quest steps**, and small **discovery**
  bonuses (each ice chip / trail marker). Guarding, talking your way out,
  and parley routes never yield less total XP than fighting — the system
  rewards finishing encounters, not grinding them.
- Level-ups fully heal, raise stats, and unlock one new battle command.
- Party of one in Act 1 (Joseph); the schema supports more members later.

### Curve (tuned so the boss lands at Level 4)

| Level | Cumulative XP | Stat gains on reaching it | Command unlocked |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | HP 32 / ATK 9 / DEF 3 / SPD 12 (current demo stats) | Attack, Guard |
| 2 | 20 | +6 HP, +2 ATK, +1 DEF, +1 SPD | **Focus** — next attack ×1.5, but gauge refills 25% slower once |
| 3 | 45 | +6 HP, +2 ATK, +1 DEF, +1 SPD | **Cactus Guard** — guard also reflects 2 damage |
| 4 | 75 | +6 HP, +2 ATK, +1 DEF, +1 SPD | **Second Wind** — heal 30% max HP, once per battle |
| 5 | 110 | +6 HP, +2 ATK, +1 DEF, +1 SPD | **Sandstep** — instantly refill 50% gauge (long cooldown) |

### XP awards (Act 1)

| Source | XP | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Scarab | 8 | Tutorial + trail + mine encounters |
| Buzzard | 10 | Trail zones |
| Gila monster | 14 | Trail miniboss-flavored regular |
| Jackrabbit (optional) | 12 | Or 12 XP for the trade resolution |
| Foreman Scarab | 30 | Mid-boss |
| Dust Queen | 60 | Fight **or** parley route |
| Quest: crash-site scene | 5 | |
| Quest: three ice chips on the flats | 5 × 3 | Discovery bonuses |
| Quest: Dusty's trade | 10 | |
| Quest: mine-cart lever | 10 | |
| Side quest: feed & water the chickens | 10 | Optional, homestead only |

Mandatory-path total ≈ **8 + 8 + 10 + 14 + 30 + 60 + 55 quest/discovery
= ~165 XP** → comfortably Level 4 before the Queen, Level 5 exactly if the
player also does the jackrabbit encounter and stray brood fights. A rushed
player who skips everything optional still reaches the boss at Level 4's
threshold (140+); a completionist caps at 5 — no grinding loop needed
inside a 30-minute act.

### Implementation notes (engineering contract, not yet built)
- `src/core/progression.ts` — pure, tested module: `levelForXp(xp)`,
  `xpToNext(xp)`, `statsForLevel(level)`, `awardXp(state, source)`.
  Deterministic tables (above), no engine imports — same pattern as
  `atb.ts`.
- `AtbBattle` already deep-copies stats, so leveled stats plug straight
  into battle construction; new commands extend the existing
  `action` union (`"focus" | "second-wind" | ...`) with the same
  event-driven tests.
- Tests to write: curve monotonicity, exact thresholds, stat table
  lookups, full-heal on level-up, act-1 XP budget simulation (mandatory
  path ends in [L4, L5)).

  oasisMap.ts`); the demo battle is Beat 2's tutorial battle.
- The hero is Joseph everywhere a name appears.
- **Retcon:** Beat 2 originally starred Sahra, a desert hermit
  (`src/core/scripts/sahra.ts` / `sahraAct1.ts`, since deleted). She's
  been replaced with Joseph's parents, John and Pamela
  (`src/core/scripts/homeAct1.ts`) — see the cast table and Beat 2 above.

  dialogue instead. Sahra isn't cut from the game; she's free to appear
  fresh, later, unconnected to Act 1 — see `docs/STORY_ACTS3-7.md` Act 5.

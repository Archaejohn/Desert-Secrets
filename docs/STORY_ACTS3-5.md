# Desert Secrets — Acts 3–5 (long-term plan)

The back half of the game answers one question: **how do you catch a
penguin who doesn't want to be caught?** Answer: you learn what he loves,
and you cook it.

## Act 3 — "The Sunless Sea" (~35 min)

Joseph and Slither chase the two penguins through underground waterways
into a bioluminescent cavern ocean beneath the glacier — mint-glow reefs,
kelp forests, drifting ice floes. New traversal flavor: raft/floe hopping;
Slither swims ahead as the scout.

- The penguins are *fast* in water; every near-catch fails comically.
- Mid-act: the flooded ruins of the sun-temple Sahra spoke of — the desert
  wasn't hiding treasure, it was hiding **an ecosystem**: the ice, the
  sea, and things that have lived down here since the Mojave was ocean.
- **Climax:** they corner not Piggy but the gray chick — **Fluffball** —
  exhausted and hiding. Fluffball can talk (to everyone's surprise,
  including Fluffball's) and, once fed and calmed, **tells the secret**:
  Piggy isn't lost anymore. He's *playing*. And there are exactly four
  things Piggy loves more than the chase — **fish, stinky socks, and
  pizza — but only pizza with oranges and seaweed on it.**
- Fluffball joins as a non-combat companion (rides in the pack, comments,
  waddles alongside in camp scenes).

**End card: ACT 4 — THE GATHERING.**

## Act 4 — "The Gathering" (~40 min)

A collect-quest act that deliberately reuses and reopens the whole map —
surface and underworld — with the shortcuts earned in Acts 1–2.

The four loves (each a themed mini-arc):

1. **Fish** — caught in the Sunless Sea; a fishing minigame off an ice
   floe. The biggest one keeps stealing the lure (mini-boss: the Lurker).
2. **Stinky socks** — the miners' camp laundry line. Mo, Edda and Gus
   demand a favor first (clear the galleries' nest). The socks are so
   stinky they get their own inventory slot and NPCs react to carrying
   them.
3. **Oranges** — grown by Sahra in a hidden grove behind the oasis; she
   trades them for news of the world below (dialogue where what you did in
   Acts 1–3 changes her lines).
4. **Seaweed** — mint kelp from the reef; guarded by crystal crawlers that
   turn out to *farm* it. You don't fight them — you trade (Slither
   negotiates; thorns diplomacy).

Meanwhile every zone shows frost spreading UP toward the surface — the
stakes for Act 5: the desert and the ice are bleeding into each other, and
Piggy is somehow the key.

**End card: ACT 5 — LA PIZZERIA SOTTERRANEA.**

## Act 5 — "La Pizzeria Sotterranea" (~35 min)

Deep beneath everything, where the miners swore they smelled tomato pie:
a **mysterious underground pizza restaurant**, carved into the temple's
kitchens, run by **Chef Testudo** — an ancient tortoise who has been
perfecting one recipe since the Mojave had a coastline. The restaurant is
lit by lava vents; the tables are set for guests who stopped coming three
thousand years ago.

- **The bake**: a cooking sequence (timing minigame) assembling the exact
  pizza — dough, tomato, **oranges**, **seaweed**, with the **fish** on
  the side and the **socks** hung by the door (ambience matters).
- The smell travels the tunnels. Piggy arrives at a waddling sprint,
  Fluffball vouches, and Piggy is finally, gently, *caught* — mid-bite.
- **The reveal**: Testudo explains the desert's secret in full. The
  glacier is the last of the old ocean, kept asleep under the sand; it has
  been waking since the crash. Piggy's frost isn't a fluke — emperor
  penguins were the old ocean's favorites, and the ice *remembers*. It
  followed him because it wanted to go home.
- **Finale**: Joseph, Slither, Fluffball and a very full Piggy carry a
  seed of the ice to the surface at dawn. Rosa is waiting on Highway 95.
  The oasis is a little bigger than it used to be. Piggy stays until the
  transport comes — then, at the last second, the player chooses: send him
  to the breeding program, or let two penguins keep a desert's secret.

## Production notes

- Each act keeps the established pipeline: palette-locked generated art,
  contracts in docs/CONTRACTS.md, core rules pure + unit-tested, BFS-
  verified maps, and a full headless e2e per act.
- New systems queue: inventory items with world reactions (Act 4), fishing
  + cooking minigames (Acts 4–5), companion banter system (Act 3+),
  swimming/floe traversal (Act 3).
- Fluffball's "secret" list (fish, stinky socks, pizza with oranges and
  seaweed) is canon from this document forward — Act 2's miners and
  Slither already smell/mention pieces of it.
